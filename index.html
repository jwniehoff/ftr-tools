FAQ

What is your refund policy?

All sales are final, which is standard for downloadable trading software. If you want to judge the build quality first, Watermark is free and PDH/L at $49 is built and licensed to the same standard as the flagships. Questions before you buy? Email support and ask anything.

How is the license delivered?

Immediately. Checkout delivers your import file right away with a three-day open window built in, so you can be on your charts within minutes while your permanent license is activated to the NinjaTrader login email you provide at checkout. No waiting, no gap in access, and because the license lives on your NinjaTrader account, it follows you to any machine you log into.

How do I install an indicator?

Tools > Import > NinjaScript Add-On in NinjaTrader 8, select the downloaded .zip, restart NinjaTrader. Every purchase includes a step-by-step setup guide with screenshots.

What version of NinjaTrader do I need?

NinjaTrader 8 (any recent build) on Windows. These tools do not support NinjaTrader 7.

Do updates cost extra?

No. Lifetime licenses include every update to that product. Improvements ship to all customers as new importable versions.

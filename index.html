FAQ

How does the Quick Draw monthly option work?

Quick Draw is also available for $19 per month with full features, so you can run it in your live workflow before committing to the lifetime license. Cancel anytime and the license simply expires at the end of the billing period. Monthly payments do not apply toward the lifetime price. The monthly option is available for Quick Draw only.

What is your refund policy?

All sales are final, which is standard for downloadable trading software. If you want to evaluate Quick Draw before committing, the $19 monthly option gives you the full product with no long-term obligation. Questions before you buy? Email support and ask anything.

How is the license delivered?

Immediately. Checkout delivers your import file right away with a three-day open window built in, so you can be on your charts within minutes while your permanent license is activated against your Machine ID through NinjaTrader's vendor licensing system. No waiting, no gap in access.

How do I install an indicator?

Tools > Import > NinjaScript Add-On in NinjaTrader 8, select the downloaded .zip, restart NinjaTrader. Every purchase includes a step-by-step setup guide with screenshots.

What version of NinjaTrader do I need?

NinjaTrader 8 (any recent build) on Windows. These tools do not support NinjaTrader 7.

Do updates cost extra?

No. Lifetime licenses include every update to that product. Improvements ship to all customers as new importable versions.

Something else? Email support@futurestradingrevolution.com and ask. Support goes to the person who built the tools. Full disclosures here.
